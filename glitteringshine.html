Glittering Shine

This is a poem written by Rinkan Rohit Jena

The sun rises daily with the glittering shine,
Every day , it says you are only mine

Searched whole world from Russia to Malesiya ,
Being unknown , You are from only my beautiful Asia.

Souls had rights and mind had fights ,over nights ,
All calmed down, when you came with the cool moon lights .

You are in my brain and you are in my dreams,
You are also in my heart and flowing in these blood streams .

Might You find my presence as super-tragical ,
But trust me ,your voice for me is just more than magical .

You are so precious and You are so true ,
Is it love or you will make it whew.

Sitting alone in this gigantic sight ,
As you are shining , my twilight .

Staying apart is just becoming like night ,
I wish you can come as my love of light .

Visit me once and be a part of mine ,
As you are only and only my little Divine .

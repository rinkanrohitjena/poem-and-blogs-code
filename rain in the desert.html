Rain In The Desert

why this piece of writing is here

The word rain is here more powerful than you think after
reading it you can know the meaning

7th july 2019

It was raining and i was the only one standing on the terrein,
Fully got wet and looked around for spoting the spot to come back again.

Time lost its mind and mind its tracks as i was lost,
This was the rain which made me to come back at any cost .
